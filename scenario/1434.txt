A first approach.
On the one hand, the existing urban structure that stands out by the chaotic and masked ordering of the dwellings, within a well-sized network of roads. These mixed dwellings include self-built houses and catalog houses. The area is well stocked with infrastructure, multilevel education, and nearby train stop. It highlights the lack of any public space.
The proposal aims to form a new interior structure of apple without losing endogenous chaos. First aligning itself to the main roads, making room for the creation of spaces for recreation at the housing level, at the neighboring level, and at the community level. Groupings of houses are created thus enabling the future characterization of these by the inhabitants. New streets are created where it is possible the appearance of new uses ... in process
