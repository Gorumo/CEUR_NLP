The organization plan proposed is based on the observation of the immediate surroundings of this composition by observing the topological behavior of housing around between these buildings and public roads. It was proposed to continue crossing spaces observed in continuous habitations to study site, targeting the free movement of its residents, intensifying communication and protection of its residents in order to make the vigilant resident population of space itself (an idea championed by Jane Jacobs on the urban agglomeration). The proposed model considers, for this type of situation, the concept of urban vitality is essential for the healthy functioning of this fragile environment.
