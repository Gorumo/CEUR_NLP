The district is designed with a clustering approach. The following points are key to the strategy of the design:
1. Prioritize view to the sea front
2. Avoid Urban Heat Island
3.Maximum wind potential utilisation
4. Avoid street noise in residential area
5. Highest accessibility - Open community clusters
6. Space consideration for future planning
7. Balanced mix of small,high rise and commercial buildings in clusters
8. Space for recreational activities
9.Energy distribution if renewable sources are involved
10. Not too close to the water front - disaster management
