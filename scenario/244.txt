-Taller buildings form the perimeter to create a buffer from the rest of the city and a sense of enclosed safe space around community 
- Buildings grow smaller from outside to inside to draw people into the center where small buildings work on a more human scale 
- Defined streets allow ease of access within the community and connectivity to the outside city
-at the center is the community space or hub, which gives the community a sense of place and focus, a quiet retreat from the busy city beyond
