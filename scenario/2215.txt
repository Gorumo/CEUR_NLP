The coast should be a green park way which surround it the waterfront and maritime edge, providing a bioclimatic equilibrium between the city and the sea.  The park will be suitable for bycicle paths, recreational areas, guaranteeing the walkability. The green border also, will allow protect the urban tissue from the sea level rise or other climatic phenomena. The proposal is based on the idea of compact city where prevails a mix use like business, commercial in the lower levels and always complementing with housing, avoiding the zoning idea and the specialized use areas. The housing typologies will overlap in order to create a dynamic social enviroment. The proposal is in work in progress and needs to be updated little by little.
