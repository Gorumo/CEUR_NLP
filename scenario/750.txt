This design caters for ventilation throughout the entire area, given the type of settlement, ventilation is important. It also caters for a sizable recreational area in the center of the village. Two entrances are in place for access to both roads. Tall buildings lie on the outside of the village creating a sense of privacy for residents. Also when entering vehicles have to make a left or right, this also creates some privacy. Adequate spaces are between the tall buildings to allow for air circulation also.
