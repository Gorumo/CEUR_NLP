New layout of the housing scheme triggers social interaction and circulation in newly created area. Cutting through the site creating new streets that have multiple purposes among others providing the inward circulation from houses to the central square by small streets where houses have back yards and therefore people can access those tiny streets. Small to big (small corridors to big square) what would be the center of social life in this area, attracting people not only from neighborhood, but also from the outside. Furthermore larger streets inside the neighborhood provide the access for people from neighboring areas.
