-  two primary roads enter into the site from both the main roads. They bisect at the "community gathering space".

- arrangement of houses on the basis of height. i.e., taller houses are away from the main road thereby facilitating views and ventilation into the plot.

- shortest houses have been placed at street corner.As compared to a taller house in its place, it would make the turning of a vehicle easier.

- Similar shapes have been identified as similar income groups, which have been tried to group together.

- space for community gathering has been provided. It is adjacent to the primary road within the site and at a central location from all houses. Also, only one of its side opens to the primary road so its safe for children to play. This space can also be used for future expansion.
