The set of houses must have access from street to the parallel streets to provide equal access to every house. To the northeast we must place the highest houses, they protect other buildings from the prevailing winds. The houses should be located on the edge of the block to create a fresh microclimate inside. The interior fulfills the function of a common green area for recreation; For the community, especially for the children. The houses are oriented to the south, so in the southern hemisphere they are cooler. There is an access to the dead end which is located on the street opposite. The set of houses also has exits to each access to the opposite streets.
