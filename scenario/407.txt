The concept here intends to respond on the urban scale as well as the architectural scale. Basic principles and design parameters are porosity, permeability, visual and physical movement and correlation amongst the blocks. The core of the design is the urban response in form of spaces created on the periphery of the site which will make interact with the street and lead to an active neighborhood and on the inside the main plaza which helps the residents to interact and enrich the experience of their life here.
